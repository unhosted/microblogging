What is this?

this is supposed to be a social-network like profile/microblog

You can Post stuff here and the app can take care of syndicating those to diffrent platforms

it also recives posts you made on those platforms and updates and saves them here

it can also produce a link containing a base_url to your remoteStorage, that makes your microblog and profile visible for people

GET OPTIONS :

after the url of the app, you can add those to trigger certain behaviour

base_url=https://host.domain/path/to/storage

me=true : logs in with remotestorage

syndicate=true : atempts to connect to sockethub und send credentials

twitter={} || twitter credentials, will get stored into your remoteStorage and used

socckethub={} : sockethub credentials, will get stored into your remoteStorage and used
